AfricArxiv Steering Committee

AfricArxiv is led by a Steering Committee comprised of members from the global Palaeontology community. Their role is to provide guidance for the direction of the project, and to help with communication and outreach efforts.

  • Thea Boodhoo, Institute for the Study of Mongolian Dinosaurs |
